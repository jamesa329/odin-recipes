Pizza

Description

The classic gooey goodness you know and love, but from home.

Ingreidients

  • pizza dough
  • tomato sauce
  • (mozzerella) cheese
  • toppings

Steps

  1. Spread the dough on a pan or baking sheet that can go in the oven
  2. Spread tomato sauce over dough
  3. Sprinkle cheese over sauce
  4. add toppings
  5. bake!
