Sugar Cookies!

Description

Sweet and gooey, you'll love these cookies!

Ingreidients

  • flour
  • baking soda
  • baking powder
  • butter, softened
  • sugar
  • egg
  • vanilla

Steps

  1. Stir flour baking soda, and baking powder together in a bowl
  2. beat sugar and butter together with electruc mixture. add egg and vanilla. slowly add in flour mixture
  3. roll into balls and place on baking sheet.
  4. bake!
